# Privacy Policy for Subscription Killer

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Subscription Killer is a local-first subscription tracking app.

## Data Collection

Subscription Killer does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or upload personal data.

The app does not use accounts, login systems, analytics, advertising trackers, cloud sync, bank integrations, or external database services.

## Local Storage

Subscription data, profile data, notes, cancellation links, currency preferences, and app settings are stored locally on the user's device.

This data remains on the device unless the user manually exports it.

## Data Sharing

Subscription Killer does not share user data with third parties.

## Internet Access

The app may open external websites when the user chooses to visit a cancellation link or donation link. Those external websites are controlled by their respective providers and are not operated by Subscription Killer.

## Payments and Donations

Subscription Killer may include an optional donation link. Donations are handled by an external payment provider. Subscription Killer does not process or store payment card details.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete subscriptions and profiles inside the app. Uninstalling the app may also remove locally stored app data, depending on the operating system.

## Children

Subscription Killer is not designed for children.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
